Sending Tweets to Facebook Automatically!

If you have Facebook page for your Pligg website, or if you are considering starting one, the Smart Twitter for Pages Facebook application is an easy way to automatically send your latest Twitter posts to your Facebook page. Whenever a tweet is made, the Smart Twitter for Pages application will automatically post that tweet to your Facebook account page. This Facebook application will make special exceptions for tweets that contain #hashtags, @replies, and retweets. This keeps the Twitter-specific content on Twitter and not on your Facebook page.

For information and support, please use the Smart Twitter for Pages discussion area. Members of Pligg.com are unable to support this third-party application.
